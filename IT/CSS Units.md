cm - Centimeters  
mm - Millimeters  
in - Inches  
px - Pixels (1px = 1/96th of 1 in)  
pt - Points (1pt = 1/72 of 1 in)  
pc - Picas picas (1pc = 12pt)  
% - To parent element  
em - To font-size of parent element  
rem - To font-size of root element  
vw - To 1% of viewport width  
vh - To 1% of viewport height
